Introduction

I am a Biostatistics PhD student at the University of Florida, where I work with Dr. Rhonda Bacher on developing novel statistical methods for the biological sciences. I have a BS in Statistics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My current research interests include single-cell RNA-seq analysis and high-dimensional omics data analysis, with applications to type 1 diabetes (in collaboration with the Brusko Lab).
